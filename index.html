// About

Policy fluency.
Technical leadership.

I began my public service career as a Jesse Unruh Assembly Fellow — a prestigious California legislative fellowship — and have spent the past 14 years advancing through increasingly complex roles in the California State Legislature, from Legislative Aide to Principal Technology Consultant in the Assembly Rules Committee.

I've built a career at the intersection of policy and technology: reading and referring over 5,000 bills, advising Members on legislative strategy, and today leading Assembly-wide modernization projects that integrate artificial intelligence, digitize paper-driven systems, and redesign public-facing infrastructure to improve transparency and access for millions of Californians.

I am currently pursuing my Master of Public Administration while continuing to lead civic technology and modernization work in California state government.
